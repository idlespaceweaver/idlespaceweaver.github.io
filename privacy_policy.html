Privacy Policy — Idle Spaceweaver

Effective date: 17 July 2026

Idle Spaceweaver ("the app", package name dev.timwelch.idlespaceweaver) is developed by Timothy Welch ("I", "me"). This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: the app collects no personal data, and I never receive any of your data. Optional cloud backups of your game progress are stored in your own Google account, not with me.

Data collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to me. There is no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. The app does not require internet access to play. The only online features are the optional in-app purchases and the optional cloud backup described below.

Game saves

Your game progress is stored on your own device. If you use the backup-code feature, the code is generated on your device and it is your choice where to keep or share it. No copy is sent to me or to any server of mine.

Cloud backup (Google Play Games)

On Android the app can back up your game progress to Google Play Games cloud saves, so your progress survives a reinstall or a move to a new device. If Google Play Games is set up on your device, sign-in may happen automatically at startup; otherwise you can connect from the Settings screen, or simply never use it - the game is fully playable without it.

The backup contains only your game progress (the same data as a manual backup code) and is stored in your own Google account. This storage is provided by Google and handled under the Google Privacy Policy. I operate no server and cannot see, access, or receive your saves, and the app does not receive or store your name, email address, or other Google account details. The app does not use Play Games achievements, leaderboards, or any other social features.

Resetting your game from the Settings screen also deletes the cloud backup. You can review or remove data that apps store in your Google account at any time through your Google account settings.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Google Play. Payment is handled by Google under the Google Privacy Policy. I do not receive your name, address, or payment details — only an anonymous confirmation that a purchase was made, which is stored on your device to unlock the purchased item.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules reminders locally on your device (for example, when your offline progress is full). These are generated on-device; no push-notification service is used.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data from any user.

Changes to this policy

If a future update changes how the app handles data, this page will be updated and the effective date revised before that update is released.
