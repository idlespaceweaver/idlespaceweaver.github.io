Idle Spaceweaver 101

New to idle games? Everything you need, in eight little cards.

📖 A two-minute read

This is the same primer that's built into the game - what an idle game actually is, how progress works, and why it's fun. No spoilers, no jargon.

CARD 1 OF 8

What is an idle game?

A game that keeps making progress on its own. You make the plans; the game does the labour - like a garden that grows while you're not looking.

CARD 2 OF 8

This game plays itself. Mostly.

Idle Spaceweaver keeps working while you're away. Your job isn't to tap fast - it's to decide what the game works on next.

CARD 3 OF 8

Everything runs in loops.

Start a skill and it repeats on its own, over and over. Set a loop going, and it keeps earning until you change your mind.

CARD 4 OF 8
Welcome back, Weaver While you were away: +12,480 Ore · +3 levels · 2 quests done

Here or away, the weave grows.

Loops keep earning even when the app is closed - and while you're here there are quests, expeditions and minigames to enjoy. Away time is a bonus, not a rule.

CARD 5 OF 8

Something to play while you wait.

Waiting on a slow loop? Chart the stars, decode alien signals, fish the void. Minigames are pure fun on the side - never required, never a chore.

CARD 6 OF 8

Small numbers become silly numbers.

Resources are fuel, not trophies. Reinvest them in crafting, research and upgrades - faster loops earn more, more buys the next upgrade, and the curve only goes up.

CARD 7 OF 8

The more you play, the more opens up.

New planets, new resources, new skills - and whole new features you haven't met yet. When you're not sure what's next, follow the quests; they point the way.

CARD 8 OF 8

Play the long game.

Milestones, achievements - and one day the Loom, where you weave everything you've built into permanent power and begin again, stronger.
