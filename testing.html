How to get the game early

The game is finished and through Play Store review — it just needs testers before it can fully launch. Three quick steps, all with the same Google account you use on your Android phone:

1

Join the Google Group

This makes your Google account eligible for the test. Just click join — no posts, no emails.

Join the Google Group
2

Opt in as a tester

Do this on your Android phone or tablet (not a PC): open this page with the same Google account and tap "Become a tester".

Opt in on Google Play
3

Install and play

The opt-in page gives you a Play Store link — install it like any normal app. That's it, you're in.

Requirements: if you like the game, please stay opted in for the full 14 days — Google requires it before the game can launch properly. You just need to keep the app installed and stay in the test; play as much or as little as you like.
